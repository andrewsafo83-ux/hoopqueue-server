Support

We're here to help — reach out anytime.

HoopQueue is a basketball court waitlist and community app. If you're experiencing an issue, have a question, or need to report a problem, please contact us directly.

Contact Us

The fastest way to get help is by emailing our support team:

HoopQueue Support

We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

Common Topics

  • Account issues — trouble logging in, profile not saving, or account recovery
  • Waitlist questions — how court queues work and how to join or leave
  • Content reports — report inappropriate posts, comments, or users
  • Delete my account — request full removal of your account and data
  • Technical bugs — crashes, errors, or unexpected behavior in the app

Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your account and all associated personal data, email us at hoopqueue@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address tied to your account. We will process your request within 30 days.
