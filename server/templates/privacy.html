Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 10, 2026

HoopQueue ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the HoopQueue mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use the Service:

  • Player Name (Username): The display name you choose for your profile. Visible to other users in court feeds, waitlists, and direct messages.
  • Email Address: Used to uniquely identify your account. Not displayed publicly to other users.
  • Skill Level: The self-reported basketball skill level you select (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Pro). Displayed on your public profile.
  • Court Activity: Which courts you join waitlists for, your position in those waitlists, and timestamps of your activity.
  • Messages: Text messages you post in court live feeds and direct messages you send to friends through the app. These are stored on our servers.
  • Friend Connections: Friend requests you send or receive, and the list of users you are connected with.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device type and operating system version, collected automatically to ensure the Service functions properly.

We do not collect your precise location, phone number, payment information, or any biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your player account
  • Display your profile name and skill level to other players on courts you participate in
  • Manage court waitlists and show live player counts
  • Enable live chat in court feeds and direct messaging between friends
  • Enforce our content guidelines and moderate the Service
  • Send you important service-related communications (such as account notices)
  • Improve the app's performance and fix technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other users: Your username and skill level are visible to other players in court feeds and on your profile. Your direct messages are visible only to you and the recipient.
  • Service providers: We use Replit infrastructure to host the app and database. Your data is stored securely on Replit servers. Replit's privacy policy is available at replit.com/privacy.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users or the public.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Court messages are stored on our servers and may be cleared periodically during server maintenance or restarts. Direct messages are retained until you or the recipient deletes them or your account is deleted.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated personal data by contacting us at the email address below.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database hosted on Replit. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

HoopQueue is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete that information.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 should obtain parental consent before using the Service.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Third-Party Services

The Service displays court locations for publicly known parks and gyms across California, including locations of third-party businesses such as fitness centers. HoopQueue is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or partnered with any of these businesses. References to business names are for informational and identification purposes only.

The Service uses Apple Maps or Google Maps on your device to display court locations. Your use of those map features is subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we will notify users through the app. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
