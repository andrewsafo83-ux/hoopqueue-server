HoopQueue — App Reviewer Access Guide

Basketball Court Finder & Social App · Version 1.0

No login credentials required. HoopQueue uses device-based profiles instead of traditional username/password authentication. A one-tap demo button is built into the app to give reviewers instant access to a fully pre-populated account.

Primary Method — Demo Banner (Fastest)

1
Open the app

The app launches directly to the Courts map (first tab). No login screen is shown.

2
Tap the orange banner at the top of the screen

A banner reading "App Review? → Tap for Demo Access" appears at the top of the map screen. Tap the orange "Tap for Demo Access" button.

3
The demo account loads instantly

A fully pre-populated account is activated. No form entry or credentials are needed. All app features are now accessible.

Alternate Method — Profile Tab

Go to the Profile tab (rightmost tab in the navigation bar) Scroll to the top of the screen. Tap the orange "App Reviewer Demo Access" button below the welcome message. The demo account will load immediately.

Pre-Populated Demo Content

🏀 Player Profile Full profile with name, skill level, and avatar
📸 Social Feed Posts 8 posts with photos, captions, likes, and comments
💬 Direct Messages 3 active DM conversations with other players
👥 Followers 5 followers and following connections
📍 Court Finder Map of nearby basketball courts with live waitlists
⏳ Waitlists Join court waitlists and track queue position

All App Features

The demo account has access to all features: court discovery by map and list, joining and leaving waitlists, creating and viewing social feed posts, liking and commenting on posts, sending and receiving direct messages, following other players, reporting objectionable content, and blocking users.
